Studying for the Life in the UK Test? Sharing a genuinely good free practice site 📚

It's built on a really well-made open-source project, domicch/life-in-uk — free, no ads, no sign-up, with 400+ questions (with explanations), full 24-question mock exams, and individual test sets. The original author did the hard work of building it; I forked it and added a few features I wanted myself, to make revising faster and smoother 🙌

What I added on top 👇
⚡ Collapsible question tracker — tucked away by default, so you just answer and hit Next. No more scrolling up and down hundreds of times.
🎯 Focus Mode — type a single word or year (e.g. "Parliament" or "1918") and it instantly builds a mini-drill from every question that mentions it, with the keyword highlighted. Brilliant for nailing one topic.
📱 Better on phones & small screens — the layout now adapts to whatever you're on.

The original was already great — I just built on top of it. Big thanks to the original author 🙏

🔗 https://ksng-bg.github.io/life-in-uk/

100% free. If it helps, please share it with anyone else revising 🙏
(And if you'd like to say thanks, you can buy me a coffee ☕ — totally optional, the site stays free either way.)
